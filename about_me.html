Who am I?

I'm a ML Engineer and Data Scientist living in Switzerland. I'm interested in both classical ML and DL. In particular, I have experience in CV, RL and NLP. In addition to Data Science, I'm also interested in CS and Math in general.

Why did I create this website?

I had two goals when creating this website. The first one was simply using it as an opportunity to learn about Web Dev. What better way to learn about websites than building one? This is why I chose a simplistic apparence for this website: I implemented it from zero, without relying on any CSS or JS framework. The second goal I had was having a central place in which to store notes about my toughts and everything I learn about. I like to read and to learn about topics both technicals and non-technicals. I quickly found out that writing my notes on paper or on loose files on my computer were bad ideas. This blog is the solution I found. A record of all my notes stored on a server accessible from anywhere at anytime.
